Advice for Very High Risk Areas

Background air pollution levels are forecast to be very high. Please note also that local sources of air pollution that the model cannot forecast may increase the actual pollution levels in your area.

Adults and children with lung problems, adults with heart problems, and older people, should avoid strenuous physical activity. People with asthma may find they need to use their reliever inhaler more often.

Reduce physical exertion, particularly outdoors, especially if you experience symptoms such as cough or sore throat.
